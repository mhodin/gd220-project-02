Who Are We?

Welcome to The Brewer’s Art! Set in a grand Mt. Vernon townhouse, we offer many temptations for your palate. Chef Ray Kumm’s seasonally changing menus feature meats, fish, seafood, pasta, and vegetarian offerings. Dinner is served daily between 5pm - 9pm (later on Thursdays, Fridays and Saturdays) and light fare is available from 4pm - 10pm. Our creative pastry chef prepares our homemade desserts.In Addition to brewing our fine beers on the premises, we offer a carefully picked selection of beers and fine wines from around the world, and one of Baltimore’s best selections of scotches and spirits.

Where Are We?

From the North:: Take I-83 (JFX) to Exit 4 (St. Paul Street). Go South on St. Paul, take a right on Chase. The next light is Charles; take a right and you will be in our block. We're on the left. From I-95::Take I-95 to Exit 53 (I-395). Take a right on Conway Street, and a left on Charles. Go North on Charles about 1.5 miles. We'll be on your left between Chase and Biddle. From I-97 or I-70::Get on I-695 (Beltway) toward I-95, get on I-95 North into Baltimore, then follow directions above.
